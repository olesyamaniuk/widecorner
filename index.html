Beat your opponent and get to their corner first!

Corners is a classic board logic game for two players where strategy, planning, and clever movement determine the winner. Your mission is simple: move all of your pieces into the opponent’s corner before they do the same to you. But every move matters, and the path to victory requires careful thinking.
Plan your jumps. Control the board. Reach the corner first.

About the Game

Corners'll really make you think - you've gotta plan your moves way ahead of time if you want any chance of winning. Each player begins with their pieces positioned in their own corner of the board. The objective is to transfer all pieces into the opponent’s corner faster than your rival.

Players move one piece per turn, but pieces can jump over both their own and their opponent’s pieces. You can actually chain these jumps together, which is pretty cool - once you get the hang of it, you can zip across the board in no time. Think ahead and you'll win faster.

Game Rules

The mechanics are simple but offer deep tactical possibilities.

FAQ

  • Can I play Corners alone?
    Yes, the game includes a single-player mode where you can compete against the phone. It's a great way to practice and get better at thinking ahead. The computer's tough but won't crush beginners.
  • Can two people play on the same device?
    Yes, Corners supports two-player mode on the same device. This allows friends or family members to compete directly against each other. It's just like playing the old-school board game version with a friend.
  • Are multiple jumps allowed in one move?
    Yes, pieces can perform multiple jumps in a single turn. You can jump over both your own pieces and your opponent’s pieces. However, you can choose to stop the chain of jumps whenever you want.
  • Can I customize the board appearance?
    Yes, the game offers several visual styles and board backgrounds. You can choose different piece styles such as checkers, gradient balls, or textured balls. Make it look however you want.
  • Can I save my progress during a game?
    Yes, matches can be saved at any moment and loaded later. This allows you to continue long games without losing progress. High scoring matches can also appear in the game’s record table.
